Our Goal:

Here at My Menu our goal is to provide an elegant ordering experience through modern integration.We strive to bring our clients the best dining experience through our state of the art menu, ordering, and pay systems

The Question:

Have you ever stopped to think about how much paper is used yearly by a restuarant?

Our Solution

Our application helps in reducing the amount of paper waste restaurants generate yearly. Our app will allow the management team to handle their menus digitally. Management will be able to change daily features or specials, without printing new menus daily.

Reasons to join the revolution

As if the paper waste isn't enough of a reason to join the my menu revolution, guests will be able to utilize the app. Guest will be able to view pictures of items, descriptions, allergy inforamtion and other functionallity.
